Our story

How Arise began to rise

Every dawn starts small. Here's the road that led us to downtown Dothan.

Called to Dothan

A vision to plant

It began with a conviction that God wasn't finished with the Wiregrass — that this city needed a warm, Spirit-led church where anyone could come as they are. The call was clear: stay, put down roots, and plant something new in Dothan.

Gathering a launch team

Laying the foundations

Before a single gathering, there was a pre-launch season of prayer. A small launch team began meeting, dreaming, and asking God for direction — laying spiritual foundations one conversation and one prayer at a time.

Meeting at Mural City Coffee

Fellowship over coffee and Scripture

We found a home upstairs at Mural City Coffee in downtown Dothan. It's where we gather now — for fellowship, prayer, and opening the Bible together. Casual, honest, and centered on Jesus.

Toward launch

A healthy, life-giving church

We're building toward a healthy church that loves its city well — a place for the unchurched, the dechurched, and the over-churched to belong, grow, and be sent. The sun is still rising on what God is doing here, and there's room for you in it.
