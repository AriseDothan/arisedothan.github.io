What to expect

Not sure what a Thursday is like? Here's the honest version — no stage, no performance, just coffee, prayer, Scripture, and honest conversation as we make disciples together.

What it's actually like

This is our Thursday Disciple Making Community — casual and unhurried: coffee, prayer, open Scripture, and honest conversation as we make disciples who make disciples and build the team that's carrying Arise toward Sunday mornings — and beyond. Follow, Become, Do — lived out around a table, not performed from a stage.

What to wear

There's no dress code and nothing to prove. Jeans, boots, a ballcap — whatever's comfortable. Come as you are, grab a seat, and be exactly who you are.

Parking & finding us

Parking depends on where we are that week — text Pastor Brian and he'll let you know exactly where to go. Want a friendly face waiting for you? He'd love to know you're coming.
