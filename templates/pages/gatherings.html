Your first time

What to expect

Not sure what a Sunday morning is like? Here's the honest version, no stage, no performance, just coffee, prayer, Scripture, and honest conversation as we make disciples together.

What it's actually like

This is our Disciple Making Community, casual and unhurried: coffee, prayer, open Scripture, and honest conversation as we make disciples who make disciples. We gather Sundays at 10 for now, while we build the team to launch our Sunday celebration gathering, then this Disciple Making Community won't end, it'll multiply into other days and times too. Follow, Become, Do, lived out around a table, not performed from a stage.

What to wear

There's no dress code and nothing to prove. Jeans, boots, a ballcap, whatever's comfortable. Come as you are, grab a seat, and be exactly who you are.

Bringing the kids

Children are always welcome, bring them right along. We don't have separate childcare yet in this season, so little ones stay with you as we gather. Come as you are; that includes your whole family, wiggles and all.

Parking & finding us

Street parking is easy on a Sunday morning, and someone from our team will help point you the right way once you're inside Mural City Coffee. Want a friendly face waiting for you? Text Pastor Brian, he'd love to know you're coming.
