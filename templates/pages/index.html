Kingdom Rhythms · How we move

Move up, move in, move out

Three integrated rhythms shape life at Arise — how we join what God is already doing in Dothan. We gather, we're formed, and we're sent.

Move Up · Gather

Worship Jesus

Sunday celebration — gathering to worship through prayer, Scripture, communion, praise, preaching, and the presence of the Holy Spirit. It's the morning we're building toward, and it's already woven through our Thursdays.

Move In · Form

Become like Jesus

Disciple Making Communities where we're formed into the likeness of Jesus, practice His way together, and are equipped to multiply disciples. This is our core rhythm right now.

Move Out · Send

Join the mission of Jesus

Everyday witness, service, and compassion across Dothan — and serving the city together on the 2nd Saturday of each month. A people who are gathered, then sent.
